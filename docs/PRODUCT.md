# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Urban youth (Gen Z students, SMK/college level) engaging with INVENTION 2026 national web design competition. First exposure to structured waste-sorting education. Short attention span, mobile-first, visually driven. Need calm guidance, not alarmism.

## Product Purpose

KEDIS (Kesadaran Digital Sampah) is a static campaign website for the INVENTION 2026 competition under subtheme "Going Green Through Smart Digital Solutions". It educates urban residents on household waste sorting through interactive tools — carbon calculator, arcade game, community directory, and categorized guides — without back-end or databases. Success means visitors leave knowing how to sort organic, anorganic, and B3 waste from their own kitchen.

## Brand Personality

Calm, Trustworthy, Clear. Voice is a patient educator — no panic, no guilt-tripping. Speaks in plain Indonesian (not formal, not slang). Uses data and concrete examples to build trust. Lets the lime-green brand accent and nature photography carry the emotional weight.

## Anti-references

- Stiff corporate templates (overly structured, boxy, soulless)
- Pre-made website templates of any kind — must feel custom and intentional
- Overly cartoonish or childish gamification (the topic commands respect)
- Dense, information-heavy government-style layouts
- Generic SaaS cream/teal interfaces

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — the interface itself models minimal waste (clean layout, no visual clutter, every element earns its place).
2. **Show, don't tell** — use data visualization, interactive simulation, and photography instead of walls of text.
3. **Expert confidence, not eco-anxiety** — present facts calmly. Let the numbers (85 tons daily, 28x methane potency) speak for themselves without dramatization.
4. **One page, one purpose** — each of the 6 pages has a single clear cognitive job (learn, calculate, connect, play, about).
5. **Mobile-first, then breathe** — the layout works on a phone screen first, then opens up generously on desktop with the 80px section rhythm.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- All text resizable without loss of content or functionality
- Readable font sizes (never below 12px for essential text)
- Sufficient color contrast (Ink Black #333333 on Paper White #ffffff meets WCAG AA)
- `prefers-reduced-motion` support already implemented (animations disabled)
- Focus-visible outlines on all interactive elements
- Semantic HTML5 structure with landmark regions
- ARIA labels on interactive controls (tabs, game actions, buttons)
